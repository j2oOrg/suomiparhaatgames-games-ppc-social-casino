Quick answers

Are these games real money?

No. They are free-play only, with no deposits, cash-out, or cash value.

Do I need to install anything?

How can I suggest a game idea?

Send your idea through the form and we will review it.

What helps us reply faster

Small details make a big difference.

If you are reporting a bug, tell us what device and browser you used. A short sentence is enough, and a screenshot is optional.

We are a small team, so replies may take a few days, especially on weekends. We still read every message.

We will never ask you for payment details in a message. If you are sending an idea for Pop Sync, include a short note on the mood you are aiming for. We keep a running list and revisit it during updates.
