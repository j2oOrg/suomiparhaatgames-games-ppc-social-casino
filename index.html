Notes from our small team

We built this during school as a side project. It started as a list of links we shared with friends.

A lot of game sites felt loud and busy, so we made a quieter one.

We kept it to three games so we can keep the descriptions honest: dice for simple rolls, matching for slower focus, and reels for a steady loop.

The 21+ and no‑money notes are up front on purpose. We want you to know what this is before you click play.

Each game runs right in your browser with simple, random outcomes. There are no accounts to make and no payments to process, so you can just click in, play a few rounds, and move on.
